# Carrot tofu smoothie

Makes 

Ingredient | Quantitity | Notes 
---------: | :--------- | :----
Carrots | 100 g | whole, unpeeled
Bell pepper | 50 g | raw, orange or red
Orange | 1 | peeled
Banana | 1/2 | frozen
Tofu | 50g 
Prunes, pitted | 25 g |
Cinnamon | 1 teaspoon
Water | 300 ml |

Vanilla extract | 1 tablespoon  

## Method 
1. Blend on high for 1 minute
