# Peanut butter whip

A lighter peanut butter replacement for smoothie or chia bowls. 

Ingredient | Quantitity | Notes 
---------: | :--------- | :----
Peanut butter powder | 100 g | 
Water | 100 g | 
Flax seed, roasted | 50 g (1/4 C) 
Salt | 5 g (1 tsp)

1. Add ingredients to vitamix blend on high until smooth
